# ICT 2022 Mentorship Knowledge Graph

> An interconnected Obsidian knowledge graph documenting the complete **ICT (Inner Circle Trader) 2022 Mentorship**, designed as a second brain for mastering institutional price action.

---

## Overview

This repository transforms the ICT 2022 Mentorship into a fully linked knowledge base.

Rather than keeping notes in chronological order, every concept is broken into atomic notes and connected together through Obsidian's graph view.

The goal is to understand **how concepts relate to each other**, not simply memorize trading patterns.

The vault is designed to answer questions like:

- Why did price move?
- Where is liquidity?
- What is the current dealing range?
- What is the draw on liquidity?
- Where is price likely to rebalance?
- Is this displacement or accumulation?
- Does this support the current market narrative?

# Knowledge Graph

This vault is designed around **relationships**, not folders.

Every concept links to related ideas.

For example:

```

    ↓

    ↓
Market Structure
    ↓

    ↓

    ↓
```

Instead of isolated notes, the graph allows concepts to reinforce one another.

# Philosophy

Markets are driven by liquidity and institutional order flow—not by indicators.

This knowledge graph is intended to help traders think in terms of:

- Context
- Narrative
- Liquidity
- Delivery
- Timing
- Execution

The objective is to understand *why* price moves before focusing on *how* to trade it.
