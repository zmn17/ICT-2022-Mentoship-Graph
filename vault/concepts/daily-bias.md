## What is Daily Bias?

Daily bias is your **directional lean** for the day — whether you expect price to work higher or lower — derived from the daily chart's structure and candle closes. It's the filter every intraday setup must agree with. Bias is about **probability, not certainty**.

---

## Reading Bias from Daily Candle Closes

- Several **down-close candles** (bearish closes) after a lower low → **bearish bias**
- Several **up-close candles** after a higher high → **bullish bias**
- Occasional counter-candles are normal (intraday volatility / failed entries) and don't flip the bias
- Bias emerges from a **series** of candles, not a single one

---

## The Power Three Daily Candle (OHLC Shape)

Each daily candle itself tends to follow the [Power of 3](power-of-3.md) rhythm — open, manipulate, distribute:

| Candle | Shape | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Bullish day** | Opens near the **low**, closes near the **high** | Accumulation at the open, expansion up into the close |
| **Bearish day** | Opens near the **high** (rallies slightly), closes near the **low** | Manipulation up, then distribution down |

> Implication: **hold trades toward the candle's close** to capture the full daily expansion, rather than scalping out early.

---

## Bias After a Liquidity Hunt

A common daily sequence (bearish example):
1. Price breaks **below an old swing low** → removes [sell-side liquidity](liquidity.md)
2. It **retraces back into** the prior range (often toward [equilibrium / 50%](market-structure.md))
3. Bias stays **bearish**, but the retracement can **obscure** the read

When price hovers near **equilibrium** for a while, bias becomes **uncertain** — that's your cue to defer to smaller timeframes.

---

## Consolidation = Uncertain Bias

- When price consolidates around **equilibrium**, directional bias is unclear
- Rely on **intraday volatility and smaller timeframes** (15m → 5m → 2m → 1m)
- Use **"surgical strikes"** — quick entries/exits capturing a few handles, not holding through indecision
- Confirm with [intermarket correlation](intermarket-correlation.md) when a single chart is ambiguous

---

## Leverage by Bias Certainty

Scale risk to how confident the bias is:

| Situation | Leverage / Risk |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Trade **aligned** with a clear bias | **Maximum** risk tolerance (e.g., up to ~4.5%) |
| Counter-trend trade (against bias) | **Reduced** leverage / smaller size |
| Consolidation / unclear bias | Light size; surgical strikes only |

See [Risk Management](risk-management.md).

---

## How to Establish Daily Bias

1. Start on the **daily** chart — mark swing highs/lows and read the candle closes
2. Note whether recent [liquidity](liquidity.md) was taken (old low/high swept)
3. Locate price within the range: **premium / discount / equilibrium** ([Market Structure](market-structure.md))
4. If clear → trade intraday setups **in that direction** with full size
5. If consolidating → drop to intraday charts and use [intermarket correlation](intermarket-correlation.md) + surgical strikes
