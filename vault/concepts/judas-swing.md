## What is a Judas Swing?

A Judas swing is a **false move** — usually at the London or New York open — engineered to **trap traders on the wrong side** before price reverses in the true direction. It's the intraday face of the [manipulation](power-of-3.md) phase: the "betrayal" move that lures breakout/momentum traders, then reverses.

---

## When It Happens

- Commonly at the **London open** or **New York open** (session opens attract participation)
- Often around scheduled news — see [Economic Calendar](economic-calendar.md) (the news spike can be the Judas move / smokescreen)
- Early in the daily candle's development (the "manipulation" leg of [Power of 3](power-of-3.md))

### Daily vs. Micro Judas Swing (Ep 19)

There are Judas swings at **different degrees**:
- **Daily Judas swing** — the manipulation leg of the whole daily candle (e.g., the pre-New-York decline against a bullish day)
- **Micro Judas swing** — the smaller session-level fake (e.g., the post-8:30 dip) that manipulates the New York session before the true move

Same mechanic, nested by timeframe — a [fractal](fractals.md) of manipulation.

---

## The Mechanics

```
   Real bias: UP
        │
   Open ─┤
        │   ╲  ← Judas swing: fake move DOWN
        │    ╲    (traps shorts, runs sell stops)
        │     ▼  sell-side liquidity taken
        │    ╱
        │   ╱  ← price reverses UP (true direction)
        ▼  ╱
      distribution higher
```

1. Price makes an initial move **against** the true bias
2. This runs [liquidity](liquidity.md) (stops) and induces traders into the wrong direction
3. Price then **reverses** and delivers in the real (bias) direction
4. The trapped traders' stops fuel the real move

---

## Why It Matters

- Explains why the **first move** after an open is so often a **fake**
- Tells you **not to chase** the open — wait for the Judas swing to complete
- The reversal after the Judas swing (with a [MSS](market-structure-shift.md) + [FVG](fair-value-gaps.md)) is the high-probability entry

---

## How to Trade Around It

1. Know your [daily bias](daily-bias.md) before the open
2. Expect the **first move** to potentially be a Judas swing **against** that bias
3. Let it run the [liquidity](liquidity.md) (don't chase)
4. Enter on the **reversal** — confirmed by a [market structure shift](market-structure-shift.md) + [fair value gap](fair-value-gaps.md)
5. Trade in the direction of the true bias
