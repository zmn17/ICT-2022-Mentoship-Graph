## Why Session Timing Matters

The market's [liquidity](liquidity.md) behavior is tied to the clock. Different sessions build and take liquidity in predictable ways, and there are specific windows where high-probability setups form — and windows to avoid entirely. Trading the right session is as important as reading the right structure.

---

## The Sessions (New York Local Time)

| Session | Time (ET) | Trading Focus |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| **Asia** | 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM | Identify session highs/lows for liquidity |
| **London** | 2:00 AM – 5:00 AM | Session highs/lows for liquidity sweeps |
| **New York Open** | 7:00 AM – 10:00 AM | Key intraday highs/lows before equities open |
| **Intraday (prime)** | 8:30 AM – 11:00 AM (sometimes noon) | **Highest-probability** window for setups |
| **Afternoon** | 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM (trend ~3:00 PM) | Secondary setups; distinct strategy required |

---

## The Prime Window: 8:30 AM – 11:00 AM ET

From Episode 3, this is where you should focus for intraday setups:

- Highest probability for clean liquidity runs and [market structure shifts](market-structure-shift.md)
- Session highs/lows from Asia and London provide the [liquidity](liquidity.md) targets
- Drop to 3-/2-/1-minute charts here to catch [FVGs](fair-value-gaps.md) and [order blocks](order-blocks.md)

---

## The Avoid Window: Noon – 1:00 PM ET

> **Avoid trading between noon and 1:00 PM** New York time — problematic liquidity conditions (lunch hour). Wait until the afternoon session.

This lunch lull is thin and choppy. Notably, it also **builds** new liquidity that the afternoon session then hunts — which is the basis of the [Episode 9 NY PM setup](../episodes/ep09-power-of-3-ny-pm/README.md).

---

## The Afternoon Session (2:00 – 4:00 PM ET)

- Offers **secondary / supplemental** setups
- Requires a **distinct strategy** from the morning
- The afternoon trend often forms around 3:00 PM
- This is the focus of [Episode 9 — Power of 3 & NY PM Session](../episodes/ep09-power-of-3-ny-pm/README.md)

## Ep 5 Detail — Index Futures Day Segmentation

Episode 5 gives a cleaner three-part split of the equities day (New York time):

| Session | Time (ET) | Guidance |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Morning** | 8:30 AM – Noon | Main active window; reacts to **8:30 news** — focus for setups |
| **Lunch** | Noon – 1:00 PM | Quiet / choppy — **no-trade zone**, protect capital |
| **Afternoon** | 1:00 PM – Close (~4:00) | Activity resumes; watch the **1:30 macro** and MOC ramp |

### The 1:30 PM Macro
- **1:30 PM ET** is a known **algorithmic macro** that often triggers significant movement
- Watch the **first afternoon swing high/low** form around 1:30 on the 15-min
- A violation of that swing ignites a buy/sell [program → spooling](algorithmic-programs.md)

### Market-on-Close (MOC)
- Late-day price is driven by **market-on-close orders** + aggressive [programs](algorithmic-programs.md)
- Expect **small retracements**, then **acceleration into the close**

> Note: Ep 3 emphasizes the **8:30–11 AM** prime window; Ep 5 frames the whole day as **morning + afternoon** around the untradeable lunch. Both agree: **avoid noon–1 PM**.

## Kill Zones (High-Probability Time Windows)

"Kill zones" are the specific windows where setups are hunted:

| Kill Zone | Time (NY) | Market |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| **London Open** | 2:00 – 5:00 AM | FX / indices |
| **New York Open (FX)** | 7:00 – 10:00 AM | Forex pairs |
| **New York AM (indices)** | 8:30 – 11:00 AM | Index futures |
| **PM / afternoon** | 1:00 – 4:00 PM | Index futures |

For **Forex**, the **New York Open Kill Zone (7:00–10:00 AM)** paired with the **midnight NY [opening price](opening-price.md)** is the FX hunting window (Ep 17/20). For index futures, focus the **8:30–11:00 AM** prime window (Ep 3/5).

## Using Session Highs/Lows as Liquidity

Each session prints a high and a low. These become [liquidity](liquidity.md) pools for later sessions:

```
Asia high/low   ──► targeted during London / NY
London high/low ──► targeted during NY open / intraday
Morning high/low──► targeted during afternoon session
```

Mark each session's extremes and expect [stop hunts](stop-hunts.md) around them.
