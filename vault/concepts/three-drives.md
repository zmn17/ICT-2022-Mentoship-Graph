## What is the Three Drives Pattern?

Three Drives is a price pattern of **three successively higher highs** (or lower lows) pushing into a significant prior level. It represents the market **pressing for liquidity** — each drive lures more retail traders into placing protective stops just beyond the level, building a rich [liquidity](liquidity.md) pool that smart money targets.

---

## Bearish Three Drives (into an old high)

```
                          drive 3
                           ╱╲     ← highest high, into/above old high
                drive 2   ╱  ╲       (buy stops stacked above)
                 ╱╲      ╱    
        drive 1 ╱  ╲    ╱
         ╱╲    ╱    ╲  ╱
        ╱  ╲  ╱      ╲╱
   ────╱    ╲╱
```

1. Three pushes make successively higher highs near a significant previous high
2. Retail traders accumulate **protective buy stops** above the resistance
3. Smart money anticipates this liquidity pool and prepares **short** positions
4. The third drive often ends with [displacement](displacement.md) that grabs the stops, then reverses

---

## Why It Works

- Each drive is a "reach" for the same overhead [liquidity](liquidity.md)
- Repeated tests concentrate stops into an obvious, clean pool above the highs
- The pattern telegraphs where the [stop hunt](stop-hunts.md) will occur
- When the pool is taken (via [displacement](displacement.md)), the resulting [FVG](fair-value-gaps.md) is the entry

---

## How to Trade It

1. Spot three drives pressing into a significant prior high (bearish) or low (bullish)
2. Mark the [liquidity](liquidity.md) pool just beyond the level (buy stops above / sell stops below)
3. Wait for the [stop hunt](stop-hunts.md) + [displacement](displacement.md) that takes the pool
4. Enter on the retracement into the [Fair Value Gap](fair-value-gaps.md), positioned for the reversal
5. Target the opposite liquidity or the daily range objective (see [Daily Range](daily-range.md))

---

## In Context

Three Drives is a **liquidity-building** pattern — it tells you *where* the setup will trigger. It pairs with:

```
THREE DRIVES (builds liquidity) → STOP HUNT → DISPLACEMENT → FVG → Entry
```
