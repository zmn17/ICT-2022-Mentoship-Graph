## Suggested Learning Path

```
0.  Expectations           → what the series is (and isn't)
1.  Market Efficiency Paradigm → mindset: liquidity & time, not patterns
2.  Multi-Timeframe + Daily Bias → set a bias, drill down
3.  Liquidity (+ IRL/ERL) + Draw on Liquidity → what the market hunts / where you exit
4.  Three Drives + Stop Hunts + Judas Swing → how liquidity gets taken
5.  Displacement → the energetic grab that leaves an FVG
6.  Market Structure Break vs Shift + Hierarchy + Swing Points → read structure
7.  Order Blocks (+ Breaker, Mitigation) + State of Delivery → where price reverses
8.  Fair Value Gaps → the precise entry (Ep 6 has the exact rules)
9.  Power of 3 → accumulation/manipulation/distribution (Ep 9/19/37)
10. Correlation: Intermarket + SMT Divergence + Risk-On/Off → confirm bias
11. Market Structure (Premium/Discount) → equilibrium targeting
12. Sessions + Opening Price + Economic Calendar → when to trade
13. Fibonacci Targeting + Institutional Price Levels + Daily Range → where price goes
14. Day profiles: Daily Rebalance / Narrow Range / Consolidation / Sunday Gap
15. Optimal Trade Entry + Risk Management + Tape Reading + Trading Psychology → execute
```
