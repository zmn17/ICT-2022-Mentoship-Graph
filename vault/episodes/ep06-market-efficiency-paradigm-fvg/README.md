## The Philosophy (Read First)

> Smart money **enters longs where retail sells** and **shorts where retail buys**, anticipating price will seek the opposing liquidity. They trade **liquidity + time of day**, not momentum or indicators. Retail fails on **stop-loss discipline** — the very stops smart money targets.

See [Market Efficiency Paradigm](../../concepts/market-efficiency-paradigm.md).

## The Entry Model — Bearish FVG (Step-by-Step)

### Stage 1 — Liquidity & Time

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Anchor on a **15-min high** — a buy-stop [liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) pool. | [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) |
| 2 | Wait for a **high-volatility time** (e.g., after **8:30 AM** data). | [Market Efficiency Paradigm](../../concepts/market-efficiency-paradigm.md) |

### Stage 2 — Liquidity Grab + Displacement

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 3 | Price **runs above the old high**, capturing buy stops. | [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) |
| 4 | Price then **energetically breaks below a short-term low** and **closes below it** = displacement. | [Displacement](../../concepts/displacement.md) / [Market Structure Shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md) |

### Stage 3 — Find the FVG (drop timeframes)

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 5 | Drop to **5/4/3/2/1-min** and find the **bearish FVG** inside the displacement range. | [Multi-Timeframe Analysis](../../concepts/multi-timeframe-analysis.md) / [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |
| 6 | Confirm the 3-candle rules (C3 high does **not** retest C1 low). **No FVG = no trade.** | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |

### Stage 4 — Execute

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 7 | **Entry:** limit **just above Candle 3's high (+1 tick)**. Multiple retests give multiple entries. | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |
| 8 | **Stop:** above **Candle 1 / Candle 2 high** or the swing high. | [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md) |
| 9 | **Partial:** at ~**50% / internal range low**. | [Internal Range Liquidity](../../concepts/internal-range-liquidity.md) |
| 10 | **Trail & final exit:** at **external range liquidity** (beyond the old low). | [External Range Liquidity](../../concepts/external-range-liquidity.md) |

*(For a **bullish** setup, mirror everything: price runs below old lows, energetic bullish displacement, buy the bullish FVG, stop below the swing low.)*

---

## The Non-Negotiable Rule

```
   Liquidity grab  →  Displacement (closes beyond the low)  →  FVG
                                                                │
                                          NO FVG  =  NO TRADE  ─┘
```

The Fair Value Gap inside a valid displacement range is the **required confirmation**. Weak displacement or no gap = no edge = no trade.

---

## Trade Management (IRL → ERL)

| Stage | Level | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Entry | FVG (Candle 3 high) | Short with stop above C1/C2 high |
| Partial | [Internal Range Liquidity](../../concepts/internal-range-liquidity.md) (~50%) | Take initial profit |
| Trail | Broken intermediate swing lows | Only trail once a significant low breaks |
| Final | [External Range Liquidity](../../concepts/external-range-liquidity.md) | Exit at the major move's objective |

Do **not** trail too tightly — the model expects you to hold through small pullbacks to capture the full move.

## Quick-Reference Checklist

```
□ 15-min high identified (buy-stop liquidity)
□ High-volatility time (post-8:30 or session open)
□ Price ran the old high (grabbed buy stops)
□ Energetic displacement closed below a short-term low
□ Bearish FVG found on 1–5 min inside the displacement range
□ 3-candle rules valid (C3 high does not retest C1 low)
□ Limit entry just above Candle 3 high (+1 tick)
□ Stop above C1/C2 high (or swing high)
□ Partial at 50% / internal range low; trail to external range liquidity
□ No FVG / weak displacement → NO TRADE
```

## The Core Insight

> Markets are efficient — through the lens of **liquidity and time**, not indicators. Price grabs retail stops, **displaces**, and leaves a **Fair Value Gap** — an institutional footprint. Enter at the gap with a defined stop, take partials at internal liquidity, and trail to external liquidity. Without the FVG, there is no trade.
