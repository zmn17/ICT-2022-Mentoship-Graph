## Daily — the Bearish Order Block Objective

```
   daily BEARISH order block (up-close candle)
   ┌─────────────┐  high  ← next objective (not quite taken)
   │  ─ middle ─ │  ← MEAN THRESHOLD (50%) — reached today → continuation favored
   │  open ~4002 │
   └─────────────┘  low   ← this low CREATES the order block
        ▲ price delivers UP into the OB (bullish continuation)
```

Three sensitive points: **low / open (~4,002) / middle (mean threshold)**. Today's range **reached the mean threshold** → bodes well for taking the short-term high. See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md).

---

## Intraday — Judas Swing, Dealing Range, 9:30

```
   NY midnight → Judas swing sets the LOW OF DAY
   4,000 objective passed → consolidate below the daily OB
   5-min dealing range: low→high, equilibrium, premium/discount
   ┌──────┐ discount FVG / OTE = buy
   └──────┘
   FVGs across 8:30 & 9:30 candles → 9:30 EXPANSION launches the move up
```

- NY midnight **Judas swing** set the LOD. See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md).
- Buy the **discount FVG / OTE** within the dealing range. See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / [Optimal Trade Entry](../../concepts/optimal-trade-entry.md) / [Market Structure](../../concepts/market-structure.md).

## SMT Divergence Confirms Accumulation

```
   NASDAQ:  ────╲___  LOWER low
   S&P:     ────╲‾‾   HIGHER low   ← does NOT confirm the lower low
                     = ACCUMULATION of longs (not real weakness) → bullish continuation
```

- **NQ lower low vs ES higher low** = **accumulation** → the lower low was a **stop run**, not weakness. See [SMT Divergence](../../concepts/smt-divergence.md).
- Spring-like **compression below the OB** → price springs higher **out of the FVG** toward the **mean threshold**.
- **Timing:** SMT around **9:30** after an early run **before/at 7:00**.

---

## The Repeatable Read

```
Daily bearish OB = upside objective (reach mean threshold = continuation)
→ NY midnight Judas sets LOD → buy discount FVG/OTE → 9:30 expansion
→ SMT (NQ lower low vs ES higher low = accumulation) → spring to the OB / 4,000
```
