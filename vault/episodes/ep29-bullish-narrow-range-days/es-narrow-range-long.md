## Bias & Context

```
   NQ: 12553.25 relative equal highs ═══  DRAW ON LIQUIDITY → bullish bias
   both indices hold a daily SWING LOW → continuation up warranted
   Context: day BEFORE Fed Chair speaks → NARROW RANGE / low volatility
```

- Bullish bias off the **12553.25 NQ equal highs** + a daily swing low. Trade **ES** (cleaner structure). See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md) / [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md).
- **Narrow-range day** (pre-Fed) → choppy; bias filters all shorts. See [Narrow Range Day](../../concepts/narrow-range-day.md).

## SMT Divergence Confirms Bullish

```
   10:00 low
   NQ:  ──────╲___  LOWER low (below its 10:00 low)
   ES:  ──────╲‾‾   REFUSES to make a lower low   ← SMT divergence
                    = bullish → shorts are OFF the table
```

See [SMT Divergence](../../concepts/smt-divergence.md).

## The Long (1-min FVG)

```
   early: took a short-term high then declined
          → NO valid short (no FVG revisited, no displacement above 50%)
   failure to make a lower low → short-term MSS
   ┌──────┐
   │ FVG  │ ← BUY the 1-min gap (~8 candles of opportunity; close-proximity OK)
   └──────┘
        │ 2nd MSS confirms
        ▼ through FVG + secondary FVG
   liquidity above ═══  ← expansion target
```

1. Early decline was **not** a valid short (no revisited FVG, no displacement placing a gap above the 50%). See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md).
2. **Failure to make a lower low** → short-term **market structure shift**. See [Market Structure Shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md).
3. Buy the **1-minute FVG** — ~**8 candles** to enter inside or in **close proximity**; a slightly imprecise fill is acceptable.
4. **Second MSS** confirms; price trades through the FVG + a secondary FVG and **expands into the liquidity above**. See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Bullish bias (equal highs draw) on a narrow-range day → refuse all shorts
→ SMT (NQ lower low, ES refuses) = bullish → failure to make lower low = MSS
→ buy 1-min FVG (close-proximity OK) → 2nd MSS → expand into liquidity above
```
