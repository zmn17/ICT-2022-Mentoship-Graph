## What This Teaches

A quick **AM-session short** whose objective is **resting sellside liquidity at an old low** (May 16, 2022). ICT points *down* at that low while price trades *above* it — not a no-go zone, but a **liquidity magnet**. Top-down (daily → 15 → 5), he shorts off a **bearish order block + filled FVG + a fresh swing high**, reduces risk, trails, scales out, and rides through NY-lunch consolidation as each pause **builds stops that price then runs** down to the target.

## Why Point at an Old Low From Above

> Pointing *down* at an old low while price trades *above* it is **not** saying "avoid this level." It marks a **pool of resting sellside liquidity** (sell stops from longs + breakout sellers) that the market is **drawn to reach for**. It's a **scalp-style liquidity objective**, not a long-term directional call.
