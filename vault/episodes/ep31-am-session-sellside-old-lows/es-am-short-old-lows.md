---

## The Objective

```
   price trading ABOVE  ← ICT points DOWN at:
   ═════════════════════ May 16, 2022 OLD LOW = resting SELLSIDE liquidity (the draw)
        │  sell stops from longs + breakout sellers rest here
        ▼  scalp objective: run below this old low
```

Pointing at an old low from above = **aiming for liquidity**, not avoiding the level. See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md) / [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md).

---

## The Entry (Top-Down)

```
   15-min: bearish ORDER BLOCK (up-close candle before rollover)
        ╱╲  price returns to the OB
   ┌───┴──┐
   │ FVG  │  5-min: tags a filled fair value gap (slight overshoot, volatility)
   └──────┘
        ╱╲  3-candle SWING HIGH forms = short trigger
   ─────  SHORT on confirming candle close; stop just above the swing
```

- **15-min bearish OB** = the up-close candle immediately before price rolls over. See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md).
- **5-min:** price tags a **fair value gap**, overshoots slightly, prints a **3-candle swing high**. See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / [Swing Points](../../concepts/swing-points.md).
- **Short** on the confirming candle's close; **stop just above the swing high**.

---

## Management → the Target

```
   entry ─┐ reduce risk + trail stop as price breaks lower
          ▼ scale out with partial LIMIT orders
   NY lunch consolidation ─── builds stops ───▶ price RUNS them lower
          ▼ breaks through FVGs
   ═════════ meets LOW OF DAY = May 16 sellside liquidity (objective reached)
```

- **Reduce risk, trail** the stop; **scale out** with partials. See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).
- Each **NY-lunch consolidation builds stops** that price then **runs** lower. See [Trading Sessions](../../concepts/trading-sessions.md).
- Price meets the **LOD** at the targeted **May 16 old low**.

---

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Old low = sellside draw → 15m bearish OB → 5m FVG tag + swing high = short
→ trail + scale → lunch consolidations build stops → run to the old low / LOD
```
