# NQ Bullish Order Block Walkthrough (~14,110)

> **Source:** 2022 ICT Mentorship Episode 13 — *Advanced Price Action Theory In Action*
> **Instrument:** NASDAQ (NQ) futures (ES used for confirmation)

---

## Stage 1 — Bias: the Buy-Side Draw + ES Sympathy

```
   ═══════════════ relative equal highs ≈ 14,110  ← BUY-SIDE liquidity (the draw)
        │  price should reach UP for these buy stops
        │
   ┌────┴───┐  up-close candle open + FVG = upside magnets
   │  FVG   │
   └────────┘
```

- Bearish overall, but **won't short into a low** — price likely trades **up** into the up-close candle open / FVG first. See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).
- Dominant draw = **buy-side above the relative equal highs (~14,110)**.
- **ES has upside energy**; lethargic **NQ dragged higher in sympathy** (indices move in tandem). See [Intermarket Correlation](../../concepts/intermarket-correlation.md).

---

## Stage 2 — The High-Probability Bullish Order Block

```
   run-up ╱
         ╱  ┌──────┐  down-close candle
        ╱   │  OB  │  + imbalance when price moves away
   ────╱    └──────┘  + narrative points HIGHER (buy-side draw)
              = high-probability BULLISH order block
   hourly OB = TWO candles on the 15-min
```

Three required ingredients (all must be present):
1. A **down-closed candle**
2. An **imbalance** created when price moves away from it
3. A **narrative** that price is likely to trade higher (buy-side draw)

See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) and [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md).

---

## Stage 3 — 5-min Entry at OB × FVG (after 9:30)

```
   9:30 open ─┐ Judas swing (fake move) — don't chase
              │
   ┌──────────┴┐
   │ OB × FVG  │ ← BUY the retrace here (discount, below 50% mean threshold)
   └───────────┘
        │ down-close candles support the move up
        ▼ rally toward 14,110 buy-side liquidity
```

- The **9:30 move is a Judas swing** — don't chase either direction. See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md).
- Entry = where the **order block overlaps the fair value gap** (confluence of HTF OB + LTF OB + gap), in **discount** below the OB's **50% mean threshold**.

---

## Stage 4 — Management to the Buy-Side

- **Down-close candles must keep supporting** each new leg higher; a break below them warns the idea is failing. See [Institutional Order Flow](../../concepts/institutional-order-flow.md).
- The **intermediate-term low must not be violated** until the objective (14,110 buy-side) is reached. See [Market Structure Hierarchy](../../concepts/market-structure-hierarchy.md).
- **Stops:** below the OB **mean threshold (50%)** at entry (too-tight stops inside the retrace get picked off); **trail up beneath a down-close candle** only once price has decisively left it behind as fresh support. See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).
- Near the objective the algorithm makes **only a small retrace inside the FVG** before continuing — it "doesn't like to go all the way back in" close to target.

---

## Liquidity Mechanics at the Target

The **buy stops above 14,110** become **market buy orders** when triggered — filling smart-money **longs** to counterparties. That's the liquidity the whole trade was drawn toward. See [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md).

---

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Buy-side draw (~14,110) + ES sympathy → high-prob bullish OB (down-close + imbalance + narrative)
→ 9:30 Judas swing → buy OB×FVG in discount (below mean threshold)
→ down-close support + ITL intact → trail beneath down-closes → deliver to buy-side
```
