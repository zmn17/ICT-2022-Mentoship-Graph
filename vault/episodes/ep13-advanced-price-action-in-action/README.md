## The Entry Model (Step-by-Step)

### Stage 1 — Bias via Draw + Intermarket

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Even while bearish overall, **refuse to short into a low** — price likely draws **up** first. | [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md) |
| 2 | The dominant draw = **buy-side liquidity above relative equal highs (~14,110)**. | [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md) |
| 3 | **ES has upside energy**; NQ is lethargic but should be **dragged higher in sympathy** (indices move in tandem). | [Intermarket Correlation](../../concepts/intermarket-correlation.md) |

### Stage 2 — The High-Probability Bullish Order Block

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 4 | Find the **down-closed candle** left after a run-up. | [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) |
| 5 | It qualifies only with **all three**: down-close **+** an **imbalance** when price moves away **+** a **higher narrative** (draw is buy-side). | [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) / [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |
| 6 | Drop timeframes: the hourly OB = **two candles on the 15-min**; entry where **OB overlaps the FVG**. | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |

### Stage 3 — Time the Entry (9:30)

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 7 | The **9:30 NY open** move is a **Judas swing** — don't chase either way. | [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md) |
| 8 | On the **5-min**, buy the retrace into the **OB × FVG** (in discount, below the OB's 50% **mean threshold**). | [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) |

### Stage 4 — Manage to the Buy-Side

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 9 | **Down-close candles must keep supporting** each new leg up; break below them = idea failing. | [Institutional Order Flow](../../concepts/institutional-order-flow.md) |
| 10 | The **intermediate-term low** must **not** be violated until the objective is reached. | [Market Structure Hierarchy](../../concepts/market-structure-hierarchy.md) |
| 11 | **Stop:** below the OB **mean threshold (50%)** on entry — not too tight inside the retrace. Trail up **beneath a down-close candle** only after price leaves it behind as fresh support. | [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md) |

## Mean Threshold (Key Detail)

The **mean threshold** is the **50% of the order block**. On entry, keep the stop **below** it — a stop placed too tight *inside* the retracement zone gets picked off. See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md).
