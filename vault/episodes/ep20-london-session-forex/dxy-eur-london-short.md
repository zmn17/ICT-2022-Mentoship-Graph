## The Inverse Barometer Read

```
   DXY (barometer)                      EUR (trading market)
   ───────╲                             ────────────╱╲  false breakout
           ╲ 99.92 buy level                       ╱  above prev week HIGH
            ▼ London low (2–5 AM NY)              ╱    = Judas swing
            (engineered by the market maker)     ╱
                                                ▼ then reverse DOWN → short
   at the SAME instant: DXY tags 99.92  ⟺  EUR tags its false high
```

- **Market-maker view:** DXY driven down to **99.92** (a ~3-pip zone, 99.95→99.92) as an engineered **buy** level. See [Intermarket Correlation](../../concepts/intermarket-correlation.md).
- **Inverse relationship:** DXY down ⟷ EUR up. When the dollar hits its buy objective, EUR's simultaneous push **above the previous week's high** is exposed as a **false breakout**. See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md).
- Both extremes form inside the **London Kill Zone (2–5 AM NY)**. See [Trading Sessions](../../concepts/trading-sessions.md).

---

## Execution (2-min EUR)

```
   prev week high ═══════  ← EUR sweeps this (false breakout) then reverses
        ╱╲
   ────╱  ╲  displacement LOWER → breaks short-term low = MSS
   ┌──────┐╲
   │ FVG  │ ╲  ← SELL limit here after price moves away
   └──────┘     stop just above swing high (<5 pips)
        ▼ target: 50% Fib of the energetic low (then deeper imbalance)
```

1. On the 2-min EUR chart, **displacement** lower breaks a short-term low → **market structure shift** + a **fair value gap**. See [Displacement](../../concepts/displacement.md) / [Market Structure Shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md).
2. **Entry:** limit in the FVG **after** price energetically moves away (don't chase — the Kill Zone is still open). See [Optimal Trade Entry](../../concepts/optimal-trade-entry.md).
3. **Stop:** just above the swing high → **under 5 pips** of risk.
4. **Target:** Fib from the most energetic low → **50%** first target; a deeper imbalance for a full-day hold. See [Fibonacci Targeting](../../concepts/fibonacci-targeting.md).

---

## Money Management

- **Fixed percentage**, not dollars: **1% max**, preferably **½% or ¼%**
- A sub-5-pip stop lets you size precisely to the % risk
- See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md)

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
DXY engineered to a buy level (99.92) → inverse: EUR rallies
→ EUR false breakout above prev week high (Judas swing) in London Kill Zone
→ 2-min displacement + MSS + FVG → limit short (<5 pips) → 50% Fib target
```
