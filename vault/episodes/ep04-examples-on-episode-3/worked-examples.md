## Example 1 — E-mini S&P (ES), Wed Jan 26 (2-min)

### The Trade
```
   HIGH ───────────────  (range top)
        ╲   run above old high (liquidity) after 8:30
        ┌─────────┐
        │  FVG    │  ← short the retrace into the gap (~4419)
        └─────────┘
             │ break of swing low = bearish MSS
             ▼
   ── equilibrium (50% Fib) ──
             ▼
   old low / imbalance (discount)  ← cover (~4382)
   LOW ────────────────  (range bottom)
```

1. **Range:** marked the Wednesday low up to the high — "that's our range." Dropped the [Fib](../../concepts/fibonacci-targeting.md); marked **50% equilibrium**. See [Market Structure](../../concepts/market-structure.md).
2. **Premium:** the run-up pushed price into **premium** (expensive) → short bias.
3. **Hunt:** **8:30 AM** started the hunt; price **violated an old high** ([liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md)).
4. **MSS:** price broke the **swing low** → bearish [market structure shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md).
5. **FVG entry:** price retraced **into the fair value gap** — the ideal short. See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md).
6. **Delivery:** hypothetical short **~4419**, cover **~4382** at the discount old low — "the opportunity of the range."

### Journal It
Log: time from **MSS → gap fill**, time from **entry → target**, and the **heat/drawdown** you'd have weathered. Use your own charts. See [Trading Psychology](../../concepts/trading-psychology.md).

## Example 2 — E-mini Nasdaq (NQ), Thu Jan 27 (5-min) — the Counter-Example

### First run: FAILS the criteria
```
   relative equal highs ═════  ← liquidity objective
              ╱  run takes the equal highs…
   ──────────╱
              …but NO fair value gap forms on the break
              → does NOT meet criteria → NO TRADE
```

- Price ran the **relative equal highs** ([liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md)) but **did not** break down leaving a **fair value gap** → **no trade**.
- Lesson: liquidity objectives alone are **not** entries without the structural + FVG confirmation. **Wait** — let the market show its hand. See [Trading Psychology](../../concepts/trading-psychology.md).

### Later break: QUALIFIES
```
   swing low ─ ─ ─╲
                   ╲  candle trades through swing low → MSS
        ┌────────┐ ╲
        │  FVG   │◄─╱  ← short the retrace into the gap
        └────────┘
             ▼ reaches below equilibrium
   targets: swing lows + imbalance (hits all, goes a bit lower)
```

- A later candle **traded through the swing low** → bearish [MSS](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md)
- Going back from the high to that candle's low revealed the **fair value gap** → short the retrace
- Price reached **below equilibrium**, hitting **multiple discount targets** + an imbalance

## The Repeatable Checklist (Both Examples)

```
Range (low→high) → Fib/equilibrium → premium → run old high (8:30)
→ break swing low (MSS) → FVG? → short the retrace → discount target
   (NO FVG = NO TRADE)
```
