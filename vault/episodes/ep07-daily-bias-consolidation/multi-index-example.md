# Multi-Index Divergence — Fake Break Lower

> **Source:** 2022 ICT Mentorship Episode 7 — *Daily Bias & Consolidation Challenges*
> **Instruments:** NASDAQ, S&P 500, Dow futures

---

## Overview

A walkthrough of using **NASDAQ / S&P / Dow correlation** to detect a fake break lower during a consolidating, unclear-bias environment — and timing a NASDAQ long off the divergence.

---

## Stage 1 — Daily Context (Unclear Bias)

- NASDAQ daily had **broken below an old swing low**, taking [sell-side liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md)
- Price then **retraced back into the range**, hovering near **equilibrium**
- Down-close candles suggested a lingering **bearish** [daily bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md), but the consolidation **obscured** it

> When bias is this murky, defer to **intermarket correlation**.

---

## Stage 2 — The Divergence

```
   NASDAQ (NQ):  ───╲___  new lower low   ✔  (breaks old low)
   S&P 500 (ES): ───╲___  new lower low   ✔
   DOW (YM):     ───╲‾‾‾  NO new low       ✘  ← refuses to deliver
                                              = "lack of delivery"
```

- NASDAQ **and** S&P both printed a **new lower low**
- The **Dow refused** to make a corresponding new low — a **lack of delivery**
- Interpretation: the lower low in NQ/ES is a **fake break** — **accumulation** is happening beneath the surface → expect a **rally**

See [Intermarket Correlation](../../concepts/intermarket-correlation.md).

---

## Stage 3 — Intraday Confirmation & Entry

Dropping to the **2-minute** chart (the episode's preferred execution timeframe):

```
   old low ─────╲
                 ╲___  price trades BELOW the old low (sweep)
                    ╱   then rallies back ABOVE it
                   ╱    → forms a Fair Value Gap + market structure shift
   ┌──────────┐  ╱
   │   FVG    │ ╱  ← long entry on retrace into the gap
   └──────────┘
        │ stop just below the FVG / short-term low
        ▼ (tight risk)
```

1. Price traded **below an old low**, then quickly **rallied back above it** (failed break)
2. A **fair value gap** + **[market structure shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md)** formed → bullish signal
3. **Entry:** long on the retrace into the [FVG](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / order block
4. **Stop:** just below the key low / FVG boundary (tight)
5. If NASDAQ's FVG was unclear, the **S&P's** FVG could time the NASDAQ entry

---

## Stage 4 — Management

- Because bias was **not** fully confirmed, size was kept modest — **surgical strikes** capturing a few handles. See [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md).
- Full size is reserved for trades **aligned with a clear, confirmed bias**. See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).

---

## The Repeatable Tell

```
NQ + ES make new low  •  DOW does NOT  →  fake break / accumulation
→ sweep + rally back above old low  →  FVG + MSS  →  long  →  surgical exit
```
