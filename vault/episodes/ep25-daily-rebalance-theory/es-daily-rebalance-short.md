## Daily Context

```
   relative equal highs ═══  bearish OB (high should NOT be pierced)
        │  smart money SELLS the buy stops (net short)
        ▼  May seasonal weakness → bearish bias
   ─── Friday low ───────  ← logical REBALANCE level
   ███ Monday: large-range DISTRIBUTION day (opens, extends down, closes near low)
        ▼  = the daily inefficiency to be rebalanced
   old lows ═══  discount SELL-SIDE (offset target)
```

- Bearish **order block** near equal highs; **May seasonal** weakness → **bearish bias**. See [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md) / [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md).
- Smart money **sold into buy stops** (net short) → must **buy back cheaper** at discount sell-side. See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).
- **Monday** = big distribution day → a **daily inefficiency**. See [Daily Rebalance Theory](../../concepts/daily-rebalance.md).

---

## Tuesday — The Rebalance & Trap

```
   Friday low ═════  ← price rallies UP to here (rebalances Monday's drop)
        ▲  "low is in!" (trap) — really a Judas swing to PREMIUM
   9:30 run: buy-side FIRST (equal highs), NOT the prior low → confirms bearish
        │
        ▼  from the rebalance level: FVG + MSS + displacement DOWN
   discount sell-side ═══  ← target (offset shorts here; limits fill at lunch)
```

1. **9:30** runs **buy-side liquidity first** (without taking the prior low) → confirms bearish bias. See [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md).
2. Rallies to **Friday's low** — the logical level that **rebalances all of Monday's selloff**. Looks bullish; it's a **Judas swing to premium**. See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md).
3. Power of 3: **8:30 accumulation → rally = manipulation → drop = distribution**. See [Power of 3](../../concepts/power-of-3.md).
4. At the rebalance level: **FVG + market structure shift + displacement** lower. See [FVG](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / [MSS](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md) / [Displacement](../../concepts/displacement.md).
5. **Short the premium**, add, **offset into discount sell-side** — limits fill during **lunch**.

---

## Why the 15-min Looks Wrong (but the Daily is Right)

> On the **15-minute**, Tuesday's rally "doesn't look like an imbalance." But on the **daily candle**, Monday was a large down day — **all** of that is distribution, an inefficiency. Judge the imbalance on the **timeframe that produced the range**. See [Daily Rebalance Theory](../../concepts/daily-rebalance.md).

---

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Big daily distribution (Monday) → next day 9:30 runs buy-side first (bias confirmed)
→ rally rebalances the big day to a logical level (Friday's low) = Judas swing to premium
→ FVG + MSS + displacement down → offset into discount sell-side (lunch)
```
