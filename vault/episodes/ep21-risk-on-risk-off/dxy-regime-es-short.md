> **Source:** 2022 ICT Mentorship Episode 21 — *Risk On VS Risk Off Market*
> **Instruments:** US Dollar Index (DXY), E-mini S&P (ES)

---

## The Regime Read

```
   DXY (barometer)              ES (risk asset)
   ────────╱ rising = RISK-OFF   ─────╲
           ╱ flight to quality        ╲  falls in sympathy
          ╱ (bullish dollar)           ╲
   99.92 ▼ then rally                    ╲___ into relative equal lows
                                              (sell-side draw)
```

- **Rising dollar = risk-off** — money floods into the dollar (safe haven), out of risk assets. See [Risk On vs Risk Off](../../concepts/risk-on-risk-off.md).
- **DXY inverse to ES** (and EUR): dollar up → ES down. See [Intermarket Correlation](../../concepts/intermarket-correlation.md).
- Published call: DXY down to **99.92** then **rally** → ES weak into **relative equal lows** amid a seasonally weak **May**. See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).

## Framing the ES Day

```
   bearish daily ORDER BLOCK high ═══  ← level that should NOT trade through
   ── 9:30 open ──
   ── 8:30 open ──   price CAN'T rally above these → EXTREMELY heavy/bearish
   ── midnight open ──
        ▼
   relative equal lows ═══  ← sell-side draw (objective)
```

- The **bearish daily order block high** = the don't-trade-through level. See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md).
- Frame with **midnight (London) / 8:30 (NY) / 9:30** opens + **Power of 3**. See [Opening Price](../../concepts/opening-price.md) / [Power of 3](../../concepts/power-of-3.md).
- In a bearish market you *want* a **Judas swing** rally above an open to fade — but price **couldn't rally above** the midnight or 8:30 open → confirmation of an **extremely heavy** market. See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md).

## The London-Open Short

```
   ╱╲  displacement LOWER → breaks short-term low = MSS
  ╱  ╲
 ╱    ╲___ ┌──────┐
           │ FVG  │ ← short the retrace into the gap (London open)
           └──────┘
                ▼ toward relative equal lows
```

The lone clean entry: **displacement** + **fair value gap** + **market structure shift** at the **London open**. Beware **whipsaw** around 8:30/9:30 initial volatility. See [Displacement](../../concepts/displacement.md) / [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / [Market Structure Shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md).

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Dollar rising = risk-off → ES (risk asset) biased DOWN
→ ES draw = relative equal lows; bearish daily OB = ceiling
→ can't rally above midnight/8:30 opens = extremely bearish
→ London-open displacement + FVG + MSS short → run to equal lows
```
