## Overview

A bearish-bias day: the [economic calendar](../../concepts/economic-calendar.md) flags the 8:30 window, the [Power Three](../../concepts/power-of-3.md) daily candle confirms bearish bias, and rallies into fair value gaps become shorts — with the stop anchored to a [breaker](../../concepts/breaker.md).

---

## Stage 1 — Calendar & Daily Bias

- **ForexFactory:** high-impact data at **8:30 AM ET** flagged → expect volatility. See [Economic Calendar](../../concepts/economic-calendar.md).
- **Power Three daily candle:** opened near the high, closed near the low → **bearish** [daily bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md).
- Daily **high/low range** framed; **50% equilibrium** marked. Price in **premium** = look to sell. See [Market Structure](../../concepts/market-structure.md).

---

## Stage 2 — Bearish Structure

```
        failure swing (fails to break high)
              ╱╲
   ──────────╱  ╲────────  ← rally retraces but FAILS at the high
      ╱╲    ╱     ╲
     ╱  ╲  ╱       ╲___  ← breaks down again, leaving imbalances
   ─╱    ╲╱             ╲
   short-term low ✔ (sell stops taken)   ▼ downside imbalances
```

1. Price **broke below a short-term low** → [sell-side liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) hunt
2. **Retraced**, then **failed to break above a key high** (failure swing)
3. **Broke down again**, leaving **imbalances** to the downside
4. Conclusion: **rallies = shorting opportunities** (aligned with bearish bias)

---

## Stage 3 — The 8:30 Judas Swing & FVG

```
   8:30 news ─┐
              │  ╱╲  ← pre-8:30 rally (Judas swing / smokescreen)
   ┌────────┐ │ ╱  ╲
   │  FVG   │◄──╱    ╲___  ← breakdown resumes (bearish delivery)
   └────────┘         ╲
        short entry on retrace into FVG    ▼
```

- A pre-8:30 **rally** acted as a **[Judas swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md)** — a false move / smokescreen on the news
- Drilling **5 → 4 → 3 → 2 → 1-min**, the first **fair value gap** appeared in the breakdown — the short entry zone. See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md).
- **No gap even on 1-min would mean no trade.**

---

## Stage 4 — Breaker Stop & Management

```
                    breaker: higher high
                       ╱╲    ← stop just ABOVE the breaker candle
        high          ╱  ╲
         ╱╲          ╱    ╲
   ─────╱  ╲────────╱      ▼ short entry (FVG retrace)
         ╲__low            ▼ price delivers lower
```

- **Entry:** short on the rally back into the [FVG](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / imbalance
- **Stop:** just **above the breaker candle** (high/low/higher-high structure). See [Breaker](../../concepts/breaker.md).
- **Stops wide enough** to absorb the volatility "pump" around the news. See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).
- **Target:** the downside imbalances / next [liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) pool, holding toward the daily candle's close

---

## The Repeatable Process

```
Calendar (8:30) + Power Three bearish daily  →  premium/discount context
→ liquidity hunt + failure swing + breakdown  →  rallies = shorts
→ drill to first FVG (5→1 min)  →  short on retrace
→ stop above breaker candle  →  target downside imbalance
```
