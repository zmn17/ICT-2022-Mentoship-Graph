## What This Episode Teaches

How to combine the **economic calendar** (news timing) with the **Power Three daily framework** and precise price-action tools to build a day-trading method. Introduces the **breaker pattern** for stops and stresses **trading psychology** — study and journaling before live execution.

---

## The Entry Model (Step-by-Step)

### Stage 1 — Calendar & Bias

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Scan **ForexFactory**; note **high-impact** events and the **8:30 AM** window. | [Economic Calendar](../../concepts/economic-calendar.md) |
| 2 | Read the **Power Three daily candle**: bearish = opens near high, closes near low. | [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md) / [Power of 3](../../concepts/power-of-3.md) |
| 3 | Frame the daily **high/low range**; mark the **50% equilibrium** (premium vs discount). | [Market Structure](../../concepts/market-structure.md) |

### Stage 2 — Structure & Imbalance

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 4 | Confirm the bearish sequence: break of short-term low ([liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) hunt) → retrace → **failure swing** at a high → break down leaving imbalances. | [Market Structure](../../concepts/market-structure.md) |
| 5 | Treat **rallies as shorting opportunities** (aligned with bearish bias). | [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md) |
| 6 | On the **hourly / 15-min**, mark the **fair value gaps** / imbalances to trade back into. | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |

### Stage 3 — Drill to the FVG

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 7 | Start on **5-min**; if no FVG, go **4 → 3 → 2 → 1-min**. Stop at the first gap. | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |
| 8 | **No gap even on 1-min = no trade.** | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |
| 9 | Beware the **Judas swing** — a false move (often on the 8:30 news) before the real direction. | [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md) |

### Stage 4 — Execute with a Breaker Stop

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 10 | Enter on the retrace into the FVG/imbalance, aligned with bias. | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) |
| 11 | Use the **breaker** (high/low/higher-high) to place the **stop just above the breaker candle**. | [Breaker](../../concepts/breaker.md) |
| 12 | Keep stops **wide enough** to absorb volatility "pumps" around news. | [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md) |

## Power Three Daily Candle

| Day type | Candle shape |
|----------|--------------|
| **Bearish** | Opens near the **low**, rallies slightly, then closes near the **low** (downward momentum) |
| **Bullish** | Opens near the **low**, closes near the **high** (upward expansion) |

Hold trades toward the **candle close** to capture the sustained move, not a quick scalp. See [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md) and [Power of 3](../../concepts/power-of-3.md).

---

## Trading Around News

- News is either a **catalyst** (real move) or a **smokescreen** (cover for a [liquidity run](../../concepts/stop-hunts.md) / [Judas swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md))
- Expect a spike; keep stops wide; trade the **resolution** in the bias direction
- See [Economic Calendar](../../concepts/economic-calendar.md)

## Psychology (Non-Negotiable)

> Build **pseudo-experience** by studying and journaling before trading live. Accept that **losses are inevitable** — no system removes them; risk management keeps you in the game.

See [Trading Psychology](../../concepts/trading-psychology.md).

## The Core Insight

> Blend **when** (economic calendar / 8:30 news) with **what** (Power Three daily bias + structure). Drill to the first fair value gap, treat rallies as shorts in a bearish bias, and anchor stops to a **breaker**. Above all, build the skill through **study and journaling** — and accept losses as the cost of doing business.
