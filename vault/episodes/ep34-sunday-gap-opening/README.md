## What This Teaches

How the **Sunday-night opening gap** becomes a **persistent weekly reference** that price returns to and reacts against **all week** — especially in **choppy/FOMC/consolidation** conditions. ICT walks the ES delivery: down through the Sunday gap + sell-side, **FVG/OB** reactions, the gap **flipping support → resistance**, and projecting the next **draw** into an unfilled higher gap (toward a 4,000 order block).

## Extend the Gap Across the Week

> Don't treat the Sunday gap as a **Monday-only** event. **Extend the level through the whole week.** In **consolidation/FOMC** weeks price **keeps returning** to it; in **trending** weeks it may **run away** and never come back. It's a **magnet + support/resistance** decision level all week.

---

## Sloppy vs. Clean

- **Morning** was **sloppy** — few clean mechanics of entry
- **Afternoon** delivered **cleaner** setups (down to 3952.75)
- Recognizing which is which keeps you selective

```
□ Sunday-night opening gap marked (from Friday's close)
□ Level EXTENDED across the whole week

□ Trade down/up THROUGH the gap using FVGs + order blocks
□ Watch the gap flip support ↔ resistance on the retest
□ Project next draw into the unfilled higher/lower gap
□ Prefer clean afternoon mechanics over sloppy morning chop
```

Trade the delivery through it with **FVGs + order blocks**, and **project the next draw** into the unfilled gap beyond.
