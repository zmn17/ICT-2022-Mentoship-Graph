## 1-min — Judas Swing & Lunch-Low Sweep

```
   AM high 12535.75 ─── ─ ─
   Judas swing 12535.5 ╱╲  ← ONE TICK short of the AM high, then reverses
   ───────────────────╱  ╲
   NY lunch lows ═════════╲___  ← SWEPT (manipulation): squeezes longs & trailed stops
   ┌──────┐
   │ OB   │ (respected, multiple taps) + unfilled FVG
   └──────┘
        │ after 3:00 → algos SPOOL up
        ▼
   12553.25 ═══════  ← real DRAW hammered
```

- **Judas swing** to **12535.5** — one tick (¼ pt) short of the AM high (12535.75) — then reverses. See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md).
- Break lower **sweeps the NY lunch lows** = manipulation squeezing buy-stop longs and morning holders' trailed stops. See [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md).
- Real objective = **12553.25 above** (the draw). See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).
- Price digs into a **1-min imbalance**, respects an **order block**, leaves an unfilled **FVG**; after **3:00** algos **spool** up to hammer 12553.25. See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) / [Algorithmic Programs](../../concepts/algorithmic-programs.md).

---

## Stepping Out

- **5-min:** liquidity above lured retail into treating it as resistance; **no valid model setup** on the sell-off.
- **15-min:** imbalance + **optimal trade entry** rallied for buy-side toward 12553.25. See [Optimal Trade Entry](../../concepts/optimal-trade-entry.md).
- **Higher TF:** 12553.25 cleared; **12624** next objective.
- **Daily:** relative equal highs explain the bullish bias.

---

## The Selectivity Lesson

> The afternoon sell-off **ran buy-side then sold off** but gave **no model setup** — algorithmic principles don't appear in markets unlikely to deliver. Only the **bullish leg to 12553.25** was valid.

---

## PM Session Principle

```
Market consolidates through lunch → find the lunch lows → wait for them to be RUN
→ expect the move to RESUME toward the true draw
```
