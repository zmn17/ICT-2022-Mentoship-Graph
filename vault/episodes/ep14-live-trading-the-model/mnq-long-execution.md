# MNQ Long Execution — Gap Fill into 14,160 Buy-Side

> ← Back to [Episode 14 Hub](README.md) | [Master Hub](../../ICT-trading.md)
>
> **Source:** 2022 ICT Mentorship Episode 14 — *Live Trading The Model*
> **Instrument:** Micro Nasdaq futures (MNQ), 12 contracts

---

## The Setup

```
   target 14,220 ─────────────  session objective
   interest 14,180 ───────────  initial interest
   buy-side 14,160 ═══════════  buy stops (magnet / run target)
        ▲  "bust through the door" past retail 160 resistance
        │
   ┌────┴────┐ small FVG ~14,122–14,138 (continuation checkpoint)
   │  entry  │  ← long 12 micro NQ on a limit in the fair value gap
   └─────────┘
   (context: bullish MARKET STRUCTURE SHIFT already formed;
    narrative = fill the gap from Friday's close)
```

- **MSS** to the upside = permission to go long. See [Market Structure Shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md).
- **Narrative:** gap fill from **Friday's close**; want price to drop into the **FVG** for a discounted limit fill. See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md).
- **Buy-side liquidity @ 14,160** (above a pre-10 AM short-term high) is the magnet. See [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md).

---

## Management

1. **Watch candle expansion** and whether price keeps working toward the small FVG (~14,122–14,138) — not the floating P&L. See [Trading Psychology](../../concepts/trading-psychology.md).
2. Expect a shallow retrace that **traps retail shorts** at the obvious 160 resistance, then runs through into 14,180.
3. **Scale 6 contracts off** above the run to fund the position; **roll stops to break-even**; work the **14,220** limit. See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).
4. Glance at the **E-mini S&P micro** for correlated confirmation. See [Intermarket Correlation](../../concepts/intermarket-correlation.md).
5. **Exit:** changed mind and closed as price neared the **gap closure** — thesis nearly complete (14,220 still looked doable).

---

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Bullish MSS → gap-fill narrative → long limit in FVG
→ buy-side 14,160 magnet → scale out + roll to break-even
→ ES cross-check → exit near gap closure
```
