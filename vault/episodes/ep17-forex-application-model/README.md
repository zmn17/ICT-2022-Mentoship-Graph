## What This Teaches

The full intraday model applied to **Forex** (EUR/USD): read the **daily** for liquidity + bias, project a downside draw to the **1.09 big figure** via relative equal lows + a Fibonacci std-dev extension, then execute in the **New York Open Kill Zone (7–10 AM)** using the **midnight opening price**, a **Judas swing**, **fair value gaps**, and a **bearish order block**. A **10:00 ISM Services PMI** news release is the wildcard that shakes traders out just before price reaches target.

## Learn the Concepts (Read in This Order)

```
1. Liquidity            → Relative equal highs (buy stops) swept; equal lows below (draw)
2. Draw on Liquidity    → Unfinished business below → bearish daily bias
3. Institutional Price Levels → 00/20/50/80; the 1.09 big-figure magnet
4. Fibonacci Targeting  → Std-dev projection: 1.0919 / 1.0910 / ~1.0901
5. Opening Price        → Midnight NY open = short-accumulation reference
6. Trading Sessions     → NY Open Kill Zone (7–10 AM) for FX
7. Judas Swing + FVG + Order Blocks → Execution
8. Economic Calendar    → 10:00 ISM PMI wildcard
```

## The Entry Model (Step-by-Step)

### Stage 1 — Daily Bias

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Price ran above **relative equal highs** → stripped buy-side liquidity. | [Liquidity](../../concepts/liquidity.md) |
| 2 | **Unfinished business** below (unswept lows, sell-side under equal lows) → **bearish** next daily candle. | [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md) |
| 3 | Downside magnet = the **1.09 big figure** (where liquidity + Fib converge). | [Institutional Price Levels](../../concepts/institutional-price-levels.md) |

### Stage 2 — Target Projection

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 4 | Fib from swing **high → low**; a break of the old low projects **1.0919 / 1.0910 / ~1.0901**. | [Fibonacci Targeting](../../concepts/fibonacci-targeting.md) |

### Stage 3 — Timing & Execution (NY Open Kill Zone)

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 5 | Power of 3: after **midnight NY**, price **protracts higher** (Judas swing) into an hourly imbalance to form the daily high. | [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md) / [Opening Price](../../concepts/opening-price.md) |
| 6 | Above the **midnight open** = where smart money **accumulates shorts**. | [Opening Price](../../concepts/opening-price.md) |
| 7 | Hunt the setup in the **NY Open Kill Zone (7–10 AM)**. | [Trading Sessions](../../concepts/trading-sessions.md) |
| 8 | Price runs into an **FVG** and tags a **bearish order block** (~7:00) → MSS + lower high → descent. | [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) |

### Stage 4 — The News Wildcard & Target

| Step | Action | Concept |
|------|--------|---------|
| 9 | **10:00 ISM Services PMI** retraces into the OB, shaking out trailed stops. | [Economic Calendar](../../concepts/economic-calendar.md) |
| 10 | Algorithm closes a small FVG then runs the short-term low **down to ~1.09** (Fib extension + equal lows). | [Fibonacci Targeting](../../concepts/fibonacci-targeting.md) |

```

□ Downside magnet = 1.09 big figure (00/20/50/80 levels marked)
```

## The Core Insight

> The model is **market-agnostic**. On EUR/USD: the daily says **bearish** (buy-side taken, sell-side unfinished below), the **1.09 big figure** is the magnet, and execution runs in the **NY Open Kill Zone** off the **midnight open** — Judas swing up, bearish **order block**, then distribution down through the **news wildcard** to the **Fibonacci** target at the equal lows.
