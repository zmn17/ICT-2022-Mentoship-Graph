## The Three Pillars (Synthesized)

### 1. Order Block = Change in the State of Delivery
- **Bearish OB** = the **up-close candles** right before a down move; use the low + open of the lowest up-close
- **Bullish OB** = the **down-close candles** before an up move
- The algo **bookmarks** this reference to return to — it's not about your individual stop
- **Qualified only** when **displacement** out of it leaves a **fair value gap** *and* the narrative agrees
- See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) / [State of Delivery](../../concepts/state-of-delivery.md)

### 2. Judas Swing = Manipulation Against the Range
- Moves **opposite** the expected daily range to trap traders
- Occurs at **two degrees**: the **daily** Judas swing and the **micro** (session) Judas swing
- See [Judas Swing](../../concepts/judas-swing.md)

### 3. Power of 3 = Accumulation → Manipulation → Distribution
- Builds **both** the shape of the daily candle **and** the New York session low
- The Judas swing *is* the manipulation phase; distribution runs to the [draw](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md)
- See [Power of 3](../../concepts/power-of-3.md)

## Think Algorithmically

> The algorithm **doesn't know where your stop is** — it bookmarks **order blocks** and pairs orders against **pools of liquidity** (buy stops above old highs, sell stops below old lows) to **offset its net inventory**. Retail trails stops above the last short-term high; those exact stops are the liquidity smart money sells/buys against.

## The Core Insight

> Everything is one system: **higher-timeframe bias** points to a **draw on liquidity**; **Power of 3** delivers price there via a **Judas swing** (manipulation); the **order block** — a change in the **state of delivery** qualified by **displacement + FVG** — is where you enter in the **OTE** zone. Order blocks, Judas swing, and Power of 3 are three views of the same algorithmic engine.
