## What This Is

The opening "what to expect" briefing for the whole 2022 Mentorship. It **sets expectations rather than teaching a setup**: this is a **futures index-trading** mentorship, taught through **paper trading / hindsight review**, aimed at teaching a **framework** to find **one clean intraday price leg** — not to replicate rapid multi-reversal scalping.

---

## Scope of the Series

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Market** | Stock **index futures** — e-mini/micro **NASDAQ (NQ)**, **S&P 500 (ES)**, **Dow (YM)** |
| **Primary vehicle** | **E-mini NASDAQ** (faster/more aggressive); concepts transfer to ES & YM (and Forex) |
| **Delivery** | **TradingView paper trading** + **hindsight** review — **not** live-funded trading |
| **Goal** | Learn the **framework/logic** to spot **one high-quality intraday leg** yourself |
| **Not promised** | ICT's own rapid buy/sell/short reversals (12–20+ handle legs on live ThinkorSwim) |

See [Index Futures Contracts](../../concepts/index-futures-contracts.md) for cycles, handles, ticks, micros & margin.

## Handles, Ticks & Micros (Accessibility)

- A **handle** = 1 full index point; ticks are fractions of a handle (e.g., 0.25)
- Dollar value **per handle** differs by contract (ES, NQ, YM)
- **Micro contracts + lower margin** make these markets **accessible** without deep pockets

See [Index Futures Contracts](../../concepts/index-futures-contracts.md) and [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).

---

## Framework vs. Scalping

> ICT contrasts his approach with the **high-frequency scalping** many educators show (grabbing 3–5 handles many times). His focus: capture a **meaningful intraday leg** by understanding **why** price is likely to move — the **framework** is the foundation of a trading **model**.

See [Market Efficiency Paradigm](../../concepts/market-efficiency-paradigm.md).

## Honest Expectations (Read This First)

- **Demo/paper first** — don't risk live funds while learning. See [Trading Psychology](../../concepts/trading-psychology.md).
- You're learning a **repeatable framework**, not a promise of ICT's performance
- If this style doesn't interest you, ICT invites you to stop watching — set expectations honestly
