## The Forecast (Weeks Ahead)

```
   bearish daily ORDER BLOCK ═══  (+ risk-off dollar + seasonal weakness)
        │  bias = bearish
        ▼
   ── relative equal low ──╮
   ── relative equal low ──╯  = DRAW ON LIQUIDITY (magnet) → tapped today
```

- Daily **bearish OB** + **risk-off dollar** + **seasonal** tendency + **two relative equal lows** → bearish bias with the equal lows as the **draw**. See [Order Blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) / [Risk On/Off](../../concepts/risk-on-risk-off.md) / [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).
- Not hindsight — the target/bias were **outlined weeks earlier** and delivered on **Fri Apr 29**.

## Intraday Distribution (Power of 3)

```
   old high ═══  buy stops (premium)  ← smart money SELLS the buy stops up high
        ╱╲  manipulation UP makes the high (8:30)
   ────╱  ╲  displacement DOWN (distribution)
           ╲  bodies stay inside the imbalance; wicks may reach
            ▼
   old low ═══  sell stops (discount)  ← offload shorts into breakout sell stops
   relative equal lows ═══  daily draw
```

Template (15/5-min): **take a high → break a low → displacement → retrace into the FVG near the open → short**. See [Displacement](../../concepts/displacement.md) / [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md) / [Market Structure Shift](../../concepts/market-structure-shift.md).

---

## The SMT Divergence (the Payoff)

```
   ~2:30 overnight high
   NASDAQ:  ────────────╱╲  higher run (~2:35)   ← HIGHER high on 8:30 rally
   S&P:     ──────────╱╲    LOWER high vs its 2:30 high  ← does NOT confirm
                            = correlation CRACKS → ES is weak → DISTRIBUTION
```

- On the **8:30 rally**: **NQ higher high**, **ES lower high** (vs its 2:30 overnight high). See [SMT Divergence](../../concepts/smt-divergence.md).
- The non-confirmation exposes the NQ high as a **stop run** and marks **ES as the weaker** vehicle → **short ES** toward the daily equal lows.
- **Overlay:** plot ES beneath NQ, set **price source = High** → the high-to-high relationship reads like an indicator divergence, but it's **pure price**.

## Afternoon Continuation

After **1:30**, a retracement into an **imbalance + relative equal highs** offered **another short** toward the daily low. See [Trading Sessions](../../concepts/trading-sessions.md).

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Forecast: bearish OB + risk-off + seasonal + equal-lows draw
→ Power of 3: manipulation up (sell buy stops) → distribution down
→ SMT: NQ higher high vs ES lower high = distribution confirmed
→ short ES (weaker market) → deliver into the daily relative equal lows
```
