## What This Teaches

**SMT Divergence** — the flagship confirmation tool. On the same 8:30 rally, **NASDAQ made a higher high while the S&P failed to**, cracking the ES/NQ correlation and confirming **distribution** and S&P weakness — with **no indicator**. The trade was forecast weeks in advance off a daily bearish order block + risk-off dollar + seasonal tendency + two relative equal lows (the draw). SMT confirmed the sellside delivery into that draw.

## The Core Insight

> **SMT divergence is confirmation without an indicator.** When ES and NQ — normally in tandem — **disagree** at a swing (NQ higher high, ES lower high), the non-confirmation exposes a **stop run** and reveals **distribution**. It's an **x-ray** of accumulation/distribution built from **pure price**, confirming the sellside delivery into the pre-forecast **draw on liquidity**.
