# ES Tape-Reading Long (~4000 → 4051)

> ← Back to [Episode 26 Hub](README.md) | [Master Hub](../../ICT-trading.md)
>
> **Source:** 2022 ICT Mentorship Episode 26 — *Example Of Tape Reading Practice*
> **Instrument:** Index futures (ES), ~4000 handle

---

## The Read

```
   4051.25 HOD ═══════  BUY-SIDE liquidity (the DRAW / best-case target)
        ▲  next delivery = UP into buy stops
   ┌────┴────┐
   │  FVG    │  ← LONG here (price traded into the gap near 4000)
   └────┬────┘
   ─────┴──── relative equal lows ═══  SELL-SIDE taken (bought) = the fuel
   (market dropped 3× into yesterday's low, NO follow-through → upside bolstered)
```

- **Counter-bias long**: justified by a swept sell-side + stalled downside. See [Daily Bias](../../concepts/daily-bias.md).
- Price into a **fair value gap near 4000** = entry. See [Fair Value Gaps](../../concepts/fair-value-gaps.md).
- **Draw** = buy stops above the **4051.25** high of day. See [Draw on Liquidity](../../concepts/draw-on-liquidity.md).

---

## Building & Managing the Position

```
   pyramid 4 → 6 contracts into bullish order blocks (down-close candles)
        │  stop just under the low: inside lower FVG, below old STH ~930
        ▼
   retail sells the "bear flag" / rebalanced 8:30 imbalance  ← that's the liquidity we target
        ▲
   scale out 4 contracts above buy-side (market order) → let rest deliver
```

- **Pyramid** into bullish [order blocks](../../concepts/order-blocks.md) as price prints them. See [Risk Management](../../concepts/risk-management.md).
- **Stop** beneath structure (inside the lower FVG / below the old short-term high).
- Retail reading a **bear flag** and selling the rebalanced 8:30 imbalance = the **liquidity** being bought. See [Institutional Order Flow](../../concepts/institutional-order-flow.md).
- **Ignore the P&L, watch price.** Result: **~$5,000 hypothetical**, called specifically **in advance**. See [Tape Reading](../../concepts/tape-reading.md).

---

## The Repeatable Sequence

```
Sell-side swept + price in FVG + downside stalled  →  next delivery UP
→ long the FVG, pyramid into bullish OBs, stop beneath structure
→ scale out above buy-side liquidity (the draw) → watch price, not P&L
```
